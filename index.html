Interesting facts about Camels

1) Camels can live for about 40 years

2) Camel humps are used to store fat - not water

3) Camels defence mechanism is to spit at the attacker

4) Camels can run up to 40 miles per hour

5) When camels are born, they have no hump

Interesting facts about Chameleons

More than half of the chameleon population lives in Madagscar

Chameleon eyes can rotate and analyse their sorroundings in 360 panoramas

In captivity, chameleons live only for a short 2 - 10 years

Chameleons tongue can be twice as large as its body

Chameleons were first identified 60 million years ago

Interesting facts about Pigeons

    Pigeons actually have excellent hearing

    On average, a pigeon has 10,000 feathers on its body

    Pigeons are very fast, the fastest clocking 92.5 miles per hour

    Pigeons can help tel the weather

    They have helped save many lives while delivering military messages

Interesting facts about Pandas

    Pandas have great camouflague for their enviornment

Interesting facts about Cicada's

    Most cicadas have red-orange eyes

    Cicadas live for about 15 years underground

    Above ground, cicadas only live for five - six weeks

    One of their enemies is the Cicada Killer Wasp

    Female cicadas are silent, whereas males produce clicking sounds
